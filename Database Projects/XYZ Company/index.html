Project Overview

This project involved the end-to-end design, implementation, and programmatic population of a comprehensive relational database for a simulated enterprise. The architecture handles multi-tier business layers, including employee hierarchies, shift scheduling, multi-stage interview evaluations, vendor pricing matrices, and customer transaction logs.

Technical Architecture & Challenges

To ensure strict logical constraints and absolute data integrity, the operational schema was entirely structured and normalized up to the Third Normal Form (3NF). Key engineering challenges met during development include:

  • Managing complex recursive relationships (e.g., self-referencing supervisor hierarchies).
  • Developing composite primary keys across intersecting transaction entities.
  • Utilizing Python and the Faker library to build automated data generation pipelines that programmatically synthesize thousands of constraint-respecting business records.
